Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tiny Weather mobile application (the "Application"), developed by Usługi Informatyczne Kamil Chmiel (the "Service Provider"). The Application is provided as a Freemium service and is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Application, we may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (via Google or Apple login)
  • User ID (Firebase authentication identifier)

Child Profile Information

  • Child name
  • Child date of birth
  • Child gender

Trip and Planning Information

  • Trip locations
  • Trip dates
  • Generated packing lists and outfit selections

Location Information

With your permission, the Application may access your device’s location to provide weather-based outfit recommendations. Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings. Location data is used solely to retrieve relevant weather data and generate recommendations. We do not sell your location data.

Outfit Photo Analysis

If you use the Outfit Scan feature, the Application may request access to your camera or photo library. Photos submitted for outfit analysis are securely transmitted to a third-party AI service provider (OpenAI) solely for the purpose of identifying clothing items and evaluating weather suitability. The Application does not store these photos after processing is completed. The AI provider may temporarily process data for abuse monitoring and service reliability purposes but does not use submitted photos to train models.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device IP address
  • Operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash logs and diagnostics data

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Provide weather-based outfit recommendations
  • Generate trip packing checklists
  • Maintain and manage user accounts
  • Improve app functionality and stability
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service-related notifications

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • RevenueCat
  • OpenAI

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services and according to their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Photos submitted for outfit analysis are not stored after processing.

5. Account Deletion

You may delete your account directly within the Application by navigating to:
More → Account → Delete Account

You may also request deletion by contacting us at limitlessappslab@gmail.com. All associated personal data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application is intended for use by parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. Child profile information is provided voluntarily by parents or guardians solely for personalization of outfit recommendations.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. Data is transmitted securely using encryption in transit.

8. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services may process data outside your country of residence. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
